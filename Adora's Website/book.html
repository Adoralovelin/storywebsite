Meet Floofy, a brave and kind pink axolotl who lives in the beautiful underwater world! Together with her best friend Popcorn the Lobster and friendly rival Orca, Floofy goes on exciting adventures.

From racing through rivers to solving mysteries at the underwater palace, Floofy learns that true friendship means helping others—even when it means putting aside your own goals. Join Floofy and her friends as they discover that working together is the greatest adventure of all!
