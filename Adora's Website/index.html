About the Author

Hi! I'm Adora Love Lin, a young writer who loves creating stories about friendship, adventure, and magical underwater worlds. "Floofy the Axolotl" is my first book series, and I hope it makes you smile and believe in the power of helping others!

When I'm not writing, I am drawing abstract art, enjoying time with my sibling, and of course, dreaming up more stories for Floofy!
